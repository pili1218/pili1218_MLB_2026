v2.8 2026-04-14

        
Dual-strategy overhaul from 77-game empirical dataset (ML 55.8% / Moderate ML 60% / last 5 ML 80%). ML bets reinstated. Under 5-gate system added. April +3.0 bias correction. 10 Betting Decision Flags.

        
    
          
  • ML Bets Reinstated (P6_ML_MOD) — 55.8% overall (43-34); moderate conf ML 60% (40 games); last 5 ML = 80%; $75/bet at confidence 50–64; do not bet ML outside this zone
    • 
          
  • Under 5-Gate System — ALL gates must pass: Gate A (prev-day avg ≤10), Gate B (both teams no ≥5-run win last 2 days), Gate C (home SP sub-2.50 ERA + 6+ starts), Gate D (April: visitor must be ATH/WAS), Gate E (corrected est ≤6.5)
    • 
          
  • April Bias Correction +3.0 — April games avg 11–14 runs/game vs baseline 8–9; add +3.0 to projected totals in April (was +2.0)
    • 
          
  • P2 Narrowed — valid only vs ATH or WAS visiting in April (~67% hit rate); all other April visitors = skip Under
    • 
          
  • P3 Suspended — cold natural grass Under = 33% hit rate; suspended until May retest
    • 
          
  • Rain Policy Revised — skip at ≥85% (was 40%), halve stake at 65–84%
    • 
          
  • 10 Betting Decision Flags — new mandatory output: FLAG 1 (ML conf zone) through FLAG 10 (conf cap); replaces Pattern Match Report
    • 
        

      

      

        
v2.7 2026-04-10

        
Pattern policy overhaul from 56-game empirical dataset (ML 39%, Modified O/U 58.5%). ML bets permanently banned. 10 betting patterns codified with flags and mandatory Pattern Match Report.

        
    
          
  • ML Bets Permanently Banned (P6_BAN) — 39% hit rate (-13.7% ROI) across 34 tracked games; O/U only from this point forward
    • 
          
  • High Confidence Cap (P9_BAN) — bets with confidence ≥65 hit at 25% (-27.7% ROI); cap effective betting confidence at 64 max
    • 
          
  • 10 Pattern Flags (P1–P10) — empirically-derived patterns with Pattern Match Report in every output
    • 
        

      

      

        
v2.6 2026-04-06

        
Post-slate structural tweaks. 5 rules addressing variance realism, wind-ace interaction, doubleheader logic, O/U gap minimum, and slate discipline.

        
    
          
  • O/U Bet Gap Minimum (2.0 runs) — only recommend O/U bet when |Projected Total − line| ≥ 2.0; a 1.2-run gap is only 0.27 SD (~53% edge)
    • 
          
  • Doubleheader G2 Flag — DH G2 always leans OVER, never bet UNDER; tired bullpens give up more runs in Game 2
    • 
          
  • Wind-Ace Interaction — wind OUT cancelled when either SP xFIP ≤ 3.25; both aces cancel entirely (TOR@CWS: 17mph wind, total = 3)
    • 
          
  • ML threshold raised to 65% — prior 62% was too low; implied probability must exceed 65% for meaningful single-game edge
    • 
          
  • Slate Discipline Cap — maximum 2 bets per daily slate; skip discipline is working, over-betting marginal picks is the problem
    • 
        

      

      

        
v2.5 2026-04-05

        
Accuracy revision from 32-game empirical results (ML 47%, O/U 50/50, away teams won 53%). 6 structural fixes.

        
    
          
  • Mandatory April O/U Gate — explicit gate forces Moderate cap after direction is set; all 16 prior April predictions violated it (High O/U was 47.1% vs Moderate 61.5%)
    • 
          
  • xFIP Estimation Gate — knowledge-estimated xFIP cannot drive High O/U confidence; combined estimation cap (2+ estimated inputs) forces Moderate
    • 
          
  • Home Bonus Accumulation Cap — April home bonuses capped at +8% above baseline; model was picking HOME 68.8% vs 46.9% actual wins
    • 
          
  • No-Edge Pass Threshold — final win probability 47–53% → ML bet = Pass; close games went 1-for-8 (12.5%)
    • 
          
  • Low-Line UNDER Floor — O/U line ≤7.5 in April → UNDER capped at Low; every UNDER on 7.5 April line missed high
    • 
          
  • OU-F recalibration — UNDER default lowered to Low confidence; 32-game data shows 50/50 actual split; new KXF −10 deduction for estimated-xFIP UNDER
    •
